The Rules

Format

There will be a 7x7 square system in place for a total of 49 squares.



The numbers being used will be 0-6.



The home team will ALWAYS be on top and the visiting team will ALWAYS be on the side.



The Better Seed in the Playoffs will be considered the HOME team.



The numbers 7, 8 and 9 are out of play.

Rules

1. The score after 9 INNINGS will be the score that determines that gamesâ€™s winner, even if that score is a tie. The reason for this makes matching numbers beneficial.

2. If the final score, after 9 INNINGS, ends in a 7, 8 or 9, there will be NO winner that game. Instead the winnings will be carried over to the playoff prize pool and split evenly starting with the Division Series. (Not Wild Card).

3. In the event of multiple carry over, the Division series Prizes will cap @ $175 and the remaining will be split among the Championship Series and the World Series.

4. If a game gets rained out then the replacement game will be the makeup game. (If for some reason there is no make up then the regular carryover rules apply)

5. If a game is started and gets rained out before it is an official game then the replacement game will be the makeup game..

6. If a game gets rained out after it is an official game then the final score will determine the winner.

7. The Playoff Series will be determined by Score in the FINAL game of that series.

8. In the event of the final game in the Championship Series & World Series were to end in 7, 8 or 9, the prize will be determined by the previous game in that Series.



Example...

The Series goes 6 Games.

The score is 7-2.

The winning square would then go by the Final score in Game 5

If Game 5 ends in 7/8/9, then Game 4 will be used and so on.

This is to prevent any CARRYOVER.
